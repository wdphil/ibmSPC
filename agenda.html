Turning The Corner?

An expert summary of where we are now and an informed view of where we’re going.
As Chief Economist at HSBC, Dennis Turner headed a team that advised on UK economic trends within industry sectors and at regional and national level.
Throughout his career, Dennis has been involved with policy-making bodies. He was one of a small number of private sector economists who advised the pre-Blair Labour Party, and he retains his high-level Government contacts.
Dennis has extensive interests. His publications include the definitive history of Fulham Football Club, the case for an Industrial Investment Bank in the UK and critiques of Van Morrison, Joe Cocker and Ray Charles. He claims to be word perfect in the entire output of the Marx Brothers.
